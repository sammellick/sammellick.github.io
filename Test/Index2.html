About Me

Background & Expertise

Hello! I'm Dr. Sam Mellick, a research scientist and academic with a passion for data-driven discovery and innovation. With a background in computational science and interdisciplinary research, I strive to push the boundaries of knowledge and make meaningful contributions to my field.

My work combines rigorous quantitative methods with creative problem-solving to address complex challenges. I'm particularly interested in the applications of machine learning and data science to social and behavioral phenomena.

When I'm not researching or teaching, you can find me hiking, reading science fiction, or experimenting with new programming languages and frameworks.
